Volunteer Work

For the past three years my family and I have volunteered at the annual special olympics soccer tournament. I love playing sports and when I get the opportunity to help other, less fortunate players, enjoy the games that I cherish, it can be very rewarding. I also volunteer at the local food bank with my soccer teammates, friends, and peers. This work shows me how much impact you can have on a less priveled person. Every few weeks my peers and I will help out at Mckay Elementary in Portland. It is very rewarding to see the impact that a few hours of extra teaching can have on a struggling child.
